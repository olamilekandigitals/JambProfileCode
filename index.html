Welcome to JAMB Profile Code Assist

JAMB Profile Code Assist is your easy and reliable online helper for generating and retrieving your JAMB Profile Code — the essential first step in starting your JAMB UTME & Direct Entry registration process.

Every candidate who plans to register for the Joint Admissions and Matriculation Board (JAMB) UTME/DE must first obtain a unique 10-digit profile code. This code is required to create your JAMB profile, purchase your registration ePIN, print your exam slip, and complete your registration.

Our platform is designed to make this crucial step simple, fast, and stress-free, even if you’re not tech-savvy.

Generate or Retrieve Your Profile Code

What We Offer

  • Instant SMS Assistance: Correct SMS format auto-generated to avoid mistakes.
  • Easy Profile Code Retrieval: Quickly resend your existing code.
  • Step-by-Step Guidance: Clear instructions to prevent errors and delays.

Why It Matters

  • Create your JAMB online profile
  • Purchase UTME/DE registration ePIN
  • Print your examination slip
  • Access results and admission status

How It Works

  • Enter your 11-digit NIN
  • Select the correct JAMB SMS number
  • Tap Proceed and send the pre-filled SMS
  • Receive your profile code within minutes
Important: Ensure you have at least ₦50 airtime and use the phone number linked to your NIN.
