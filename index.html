JAMB Profile & Admission Support Center

JAMB Profile & Admission Support Center is your simple online helper for generating or retrieving your JAMB profile code — the first and most essential step in UTME and Direct Entry registration.

Every candidate must have a unique 10-digit profile code to create their profile, purchase registration ePIN, print exam slips, and access admission status.

Our platform makes this process fast, simple, and stress-free.

How It Works

  • Enter your correct NIN
  • Tap Proceed to open your SMS app
  • Send the pre-filled message

Important Notes

  • Ensure at least ₦50 airtime
  • Use SIM linked to your NIN
  • Old profile codes remain valid
Note: A small SMS charge (about ₦50) applies.

JAMB Regularization Assistance

JAMB Regularization is the process of making a student’s admission officially recognized by JAMB when it was granted outside the normal UTME or Direct Entry channels.

Who Needs JAMB Regularization?

  • Part-Time, NCE, OND, HND students
  • Students admitted without JAMB registration
  • Direct Entry candidates not on the JAMB matriculation list

Direct Entry Candidates: If your admission appears on your school portal but does not show on the JAMB matriculation list, you must do JAMB regularization.

Check JAMB Matriculation List
